# Fujifilm XF 27mm F2.8 R WR

**Score: 8/10**

Listing price: R6,000

## Summary

- ✅ Genuinely pancake-sized (< 1 inch long, ~84g) — smallest lens in the XF lineup
- ✅ Weather-sealed + aperture ring added over the original; great for compact travel setups
- ✅ Sharp centre across most apertures; pleasant bokeh for an f/2.8
- ❌ Noisy AF motor (audible DC coreless motor — unchanged from Mk I, bad for video)
- ❌ Soft corners until ~f/8; some chromatic fringing wide open

## Best Use Cases

Street photography, travel, everyday carry, lifestyle — pairs especially well with compact bodies (X-E4, X-T30). Not ideal for video or pixel-peeping corner sharpness.
