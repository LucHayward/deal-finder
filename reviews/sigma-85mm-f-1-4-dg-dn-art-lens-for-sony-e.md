# Sigma 85mm f/1.4 DG DN Art Lens for Sony E

**Score: 9/10**

**Listing price: R15,000**

## Summary

- Exceptional sharpness wide-open at f/1.4; one of the sharpest 85mm lenses available for Sony E
- Beautiful, smooth bokeh with 11-blade aperture — ideal for subject isolation
- Fast, near-silent stepping motor AF; works well with Sony eye/face detection
- Noticeable pincushion distortion and vignetting at f/1.4 (both easily corrected in-camera or post)
- Compact and light (630g) vs. the older DG HSM Art; includes aperture ring with de-click switch and AF-L button

## Best Use Cases

Portrait photography, headshots, still life, event/wedding photography. Less suited to architecture due to distortion.
