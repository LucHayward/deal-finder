# Fujifilm XF 35mm F2 R WR Prime Lens (Black)

**Score: 8.5/10**

## Pros & Cons

- ✅ Sharper centre-to-mid frame than the pricier XF 35mm f/1.4 at equivalent apertures
- ✅ Fast, near-silent AF (0.08s) — significantly better than the f/1.4
- ✅ Compact and light (45mm length, 170g) — easy to carry daily
- ✅ Weather and dust resistant, works down to -10°C
- ❌ Loses a stop of light vs f/1.4 — less background separation and low-light flexibility
- ❌ Slightly soft wide open at close focus distances
- ❌ Included lens hood is small and ineffective

## Best Use Cases

Street photography, travel, everyday carry, documentary — any situation where size, speed, and weather resistance matter more than maximum bokeh.

## Listing Price

R3,900
