# Zeiss Loxia 21mm f/2.8 Lens for Sony E

**Score: 8.5/10**

**Listing price: R10,000**

## Summary

- ✅ Exceptional sharpness centre-to-edge, even wide open at f/2.8; virtually zero distortion or fringing
- ✅ Premium all-metal build with weather sealing; compact and lightweight (394g) for a full-frame ultra-wide
- ✅ Mechanical aperture ring (de-clickable) and smooth 90° focus throw — excellent for video and astrophotography
- ❌ Manual focus only — no autofocus; requires focus peaking or magnification assist
- ❌ Some field curvature noted by multiple reviewers; cheaper alternatives now exist (Sony 20mm f/1.8G, Tamron 20mm f/2.8)

## Best Use Cases

Landscape, architecture, astrophotography, travel. Ideal for deliberate shooters who prioritise optical quality and build over AF convenience.
