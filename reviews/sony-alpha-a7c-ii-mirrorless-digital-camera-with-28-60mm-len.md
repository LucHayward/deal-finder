# Sony Alpha a7C II Mirrorless Digital Camera with 28-60mm Lens

**Score: 8.7/10**

Listing price: R27,999

## Summary

- ✅ Excellent 33MP full-frame sensor with best-in-class AF (759-point phase detect, AI subject tracking)
- ✅ Compact for a full-frame — 22% lighter than a7 IV — with 7-stop IBIS
- ✅ 4K/60p 10-bit 4:2:2 video, fully articulating touchscreen, weather sealing
- ❌ 4K/60p has a significant crop; rolling shutter is noticeable in video
- ❌ Only one SD card slot; low-res EVF for the price; Sony menu system has a learning curve

## Best Use Cases

Travel/street photography, hybrid photo+video content creation, enthusiast upgrading from APS-C.
